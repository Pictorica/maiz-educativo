PLACEHOLDER AUDIO FILES

The following files need to be added to this directory:

Required files:
- bg-loop.mp3 (background music loop)
- correct.wav (correct answer sound)
- wrong.wav (wrong answer sound)  
- finish.mp3 (quiz completion sound)

See README.md in this directory for free audio resources.

For testing without audio files:
The quiz will work normally but without sound effects.
Check browser console for warnings about missing audio files.
